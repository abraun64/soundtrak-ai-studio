# Data handling — Soundtrak AI Studio

> This describes how the **software** handles data when you run it. It is **not** a service
> privacy policy — the software runs on your own computer, under your control.

- **It runs locally.** The software runs on your own machine. Your campaigns, brand profile,
  and other content are ordinary files on your own computer and storage (for example, your
  OneDrive or Dropbox). **Soundtrak Consulting does not host, receive, or have access to your
  data, and the software sends nothing back to Soundtrak Consulting** (no telemetry).

- **AI processing.** When you run the system, the content you choose to work on is sent to your
  AI provider (Anthropic, via Claude) so it can generate output. That processing is governed by
  **your own agreement with Anthropic and Anthropic's terms and privacy policy** — please review
  those. Soundtrak Consulting is not a party to that processing.

- **You must comply with your AI provider's Acceptable Use Policy.** As a condition of using this
  software, you must comply with the terms, usage policies, and **Acceptable Use Policy** of Anthropic
  (and of any other AI or LLM provider you configure it to use) — including their restrictions on
  prohibited content and uses. You use your own API keys and account; **you are solely responsible for
  all activity under them**, and any suspension, ban, rate-limit, or cost your provider applies is
  between you and that provider. Soundtrak Consulting has no responsibility for it.

- **Third‑party publishing tools.** If you connect publishing or analytics tools (e.g. an email
  platform, social accounts, web analytics), any data you send to them is governed by **those
  providers' terms**. Credentials are stored on your machine as environment variables and are
  never written into the system's files.

- **You are the data controller.** Because the software runs under your control on your own
  systems, **you** are responsible for complying with the privacy and data‑protection laws that
  apply to you and to any personal data you process with it (for example, customer contact data
  you load into a campaign).

- **No warranty.** The software is provided "as is" — see `LICENSE.md`.
