Best-Practice Library

The reference collection the Creative Director and Producer draw on when creating your work — standout campaigns, gold-standard exemplars, and craft references. 95 entries, plus the playbooks, archetypes, and visual-movement guides listed below.

🔎 Looking for research, not creative? Market data, behavioural-science, and audience research live in the separate Insights Library — the corpus the Insights Manager cites. This Best-Practice Library is for creative exemplars. They're two different collections (both are in the top-right menu).

How to add to the library

Add a standout example any time — in chat, paste a link or a reference and say "add this to my Best-Practice Library" (or "add to my marketing library"). The system writes the full entry for you — what it is, why it works, and how to reuse it — files it here, and draws on it when creating your work. Good things to add: a campaign you admired, a competitor or category standout, an award winner, or your own best work. Entry shape + how to filter: see SCHEMA.md.
