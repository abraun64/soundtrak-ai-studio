## Strategic spine
- **Audience**: financial advisers + advice-firm leadership weighing tech strategy.
- **Insight**: advisers feel tech-anxiety + FOMO; budget isn't the differentiator, execution is.
- **Big idea**: an annual proprietary-research franchise that benchmarks firms against "AdviceTech Stars" (more tech · more clients · higher growth) and equips them to join.
- **Channel**: gated report + per-trend summary articles + keynote video + podcast — one research spine, many surfaces.

## Creative move
**What it did**: turned an annual industry survey into a high-utility lead magnet + authority anchor.
**The move**:
- A benchmark cohort ("AdviceTech Stars") makes it aspirational + competitive — advisers self-measure against it.
- The report ships *usable* IP: AI prompts for client comms, KPI dashboards, implementation cheat-sheets — do-it-now, not just thought-leadership.
- Layered multimedia (articles · keynote · podcast) lowers consumption friction + extends SEO.
**Why it worked**: proprietary data = un-copyable authority; utility earns the email + the return visit; annual cadence compounds the franchise year on year.
**When to steal**: a B2B platform that can run annual proprietary research in its category — pair a benchmark cohort with do-it-now tools.
**Anti-patterns**: pure thought-leadership with no utility; one-and-done (the value is the annual return); a benchmark with no path to improve.
