## Why this is in the library

Cited in the operator's Netwealth IQ Report *Creating Loyalty That Lasts* as an exemplar for **Value Driver 2: Aligned investment options**. The lesson: a sophisticated analytical layer underneath the client conversation can convert "we manage your money" into "we proactively manage scenarios you can see" — moving the adviser from custodian to active risk partner.

## The move

**What it does**: BlackRock's AI + advanced analytics platform delivering proactive, personalised investment guidance. Makes risk and performance visible to advisers + clients, empowering both.

**The move** (bullets):
- **Stress testing via real-time what-if scenarios**: brings potential market events to life → eases client anxiety, helps them understand portfolio behaviour under shock conditions
- **Proactive risk monitoring**: automated daily checks highlight emerging risks (sudden market shifts) → drives timely decisions, reduces panic-driven actions
- **Personalised portfolio construction**: considers each client's unique goals, constraints, preferences → portfolios aligned with individual values (identity alignment)

**Why it works** (behavioural science):
- **Identity alignment**: personalisation at the portfolio level reinforces "this is mine, built around me"
- **Cognitive ease**: visualising risk reduces ambiguity → confidence rises
- **Loss aversion managed via scenario rehearsal**: seeing a drawdown scenario beforehand makes the real one less catastrophic when it lands

**When to steal**:
- Professional services firms whose clients face uncertainty + need to rehearse scenarios (legal exposure, business strategy, executive coaching for high-stakes decisions, consulting engagements with risk components)
- When you can build (or buy) an analytical layer that makes invisible processes visible to clients
- Any service where "proactive monitoring" can be a distinguishing offer vs. competitors who only react

**Anti-patterns**:
- Don't show clients raw analytics — the dashboard layer is the product, not the data. Aladdin's visual translation is what makes it defensible.
- Don't claim "AI-powered" without doing the integration work to make the AI signal actually drive client conversations
