## Strategic spine
- **Audience**: advisers + investors (8,000+ subscribers).
- **Insight**: frequency builds the relationship; the brand in the inbox every morning owns mindshare.
- **Big idea**: a daily curated roundup of AU / US / EU / Asia market + business news — a habit, delivered each morning.
- **Channel**: daily email + on-site web preview with subscribe prompt.

## Creative move
**What it did**: turned daily market news into a habit-forming owned channel.
**The move**:
- Daily cadence → habitual engagement + permanent top-of-mind.
- *Curated* (aggregated), not original → low production cost, consistent value, sustainable indefinitely.
- Web preview + email capture → a distribution flywheel back to the site.
**Why it worked**: the daily habit is the moat; curation keeps it cheap enough to actually sustain; consistency = trust.
**When to steal**: own a daily / high-frequency curated digest in your category — curate, don't originate, to keep it sustainable.
**Anti-patterns**: daily *original* content (unsustainable, quality decays); no clear angle/value; irregular sends (kills the habit).
