## Strategic spine
- **Audience**: investment professionals + sophisticated advisers.
- **Insight**: serious buyers are won by substance, not surface.
- **Big idea**: a deliberately-technical podcast — hosted by Netwealth's Head of Investment Management — that runs substantive portfolio-construction discussions with top-tier CIOs.
- **Channel**: podcast + published transcripts (Apple / Spotify / YouTube).

## Creative move
**What it did**: used depth as the differentiator.
**The move**:
- A senior internal expert hosts → peer-level credibility, not a marketing voice.
- Top-tier guests (Macquarie, BlackRock, Vanguard, boutiques) borrow + lend authority.
- Published transcripts → durable SEO + accessibility.
**Why it worked**: technical depth attracts the exact audience that features can't; the platform earns "serious investor" positioning beyond transactions.
**When to steal**: when your buyer is sophisticated — don't dumb down; go deeper than competitors dare, and publish transcripts.
**Anti-patterns**: simplifying to chase reach; no transcript (lost SEO + accessibility); a host who can't hold a peer conversation.
