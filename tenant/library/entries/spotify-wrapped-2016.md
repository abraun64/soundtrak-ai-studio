## Big idea
At year-end, Spotify packages each user's listening data into a personalised, shareable in-app experience: top artists, top songs, total minutes, listener "personality." Designed end-to-end for screenshot and social share.

## Why it worked
- Makes the audience the protagonist using *their own* data — no two Wrappeds are identical
- Provides social currency: sharing your Wrapped signals taste, identity, in-group membership
- Engineered for shareability: graphics designed for vertical screens, palette built for Instagram Stories
- Infinite scalability: every user gets one; the device works for 600M people as well as it does for one
- Owns a date: "Wrapped Day" became a cultural moment competitors now have to react to (Apple Music Replay, etc.)
- Compounds annually: each Wrapped builds anticipation for next year; the platform deepens identity each cycle

## What a CD should steal
- Data-as-asset framing — if the brand collects behavioural data, that data IS the campaign material
- Annual cadence as a platform, not a campaign — own a moment in the calendar
- Design for the share format from the start; the screenshot IS the asset
- Personalisation as participation — not "10 versions for 10 segments," but "600M versions for 600M individuals"

## Anti-patterns / risks
- Wrapped-but-for-anything cargo cult: "your year in [product]" works only when the user's behaviour is *interesting to themselves*. Your dashboard usage isn't.
- Surveillance-ick: data the user didn't realise was being tracked, surfaced playfully, can flip into creepy
- Vanity metrics: "you listened to 47,000 minutes" only resonates when there's an identity tied to volume
- Format fatigue: by 2023, Wrapped was being parodied; long-running participatory platforms need genuine evolution, not just refresh

## Operator applications
- **SelfWealth / Syfe strategy deck (March 2026)** — applied as an EOFY **"Portfolio Wrapped"** for a retail brokerage: turn each investor's own first-party trading/portfolio data into an annual, personalised, shareable recap with a built-in CTA, engineered for organic social share. Passes the Wrapped fit test because an investor's own year of returns/activity IS interesting to themselves (unlike generic dashboard usage). The reusable mechanic: any platform sitting on per-user behavioural data can build an annual shareable that drives organic referral.
