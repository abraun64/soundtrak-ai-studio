# Insights Library

> 📚 **Looking for creative examples instead?** Standout campaigns and gold-standard exemplars live in the separate **[Best-Practice Library](../library/INDEX.html)**. *This* Insights Library is the **research** corpus — the market data + behavioural science the Insights Manager cites before writing an Insight Brief. Shared across all tenants (public research only).

**Maintained by**: Insights Manager / `insight-scan`. Schema: [`docs/specs/research-library.md`](../../docs/specs/research-library.md).

**Adding to it**: this library fills **automatically** as the Insights Manager researches your campaigns (and strong sources graduate here at each campaign's wrap). To add a source yourself, paste its link in chat and say **"add this to my Insights Library"** — the studio writes a short, cited research summary and files it here.

> The shared authoritative evidence corpus — **public research only**, faceted so each campaign's insight-scan pulls the relevant subset (`vertical = <this tenant's vertical>` OR `universal`). Distinct from the creative `tenant/library/`, and distinct from any tenant's proprietary data (which stays tenant-scoped — never here). The scan reads this FIRST (cite-before-fetch); strong papers from a campaign scan graduate here at wrap. Don't reproduce copyrighted text — summarise + cite + link.
>
> **Facets**: `vertical` (`universal` = applies to all tenants · or a specific vertical) · `audience` · `topic` · **`layer`** (`market` = what's happening · `human-behaviour` = what makes people tick) · `source type`.
